Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Creating an event is simple! After signing up and logging in, click on the "Create Event" button in your dashboard. Fill out the event details such as name, date, time, location, and description. You can also add custom fields, ticket types, and promotional images. Once complete, click "Publish" to make your event live.

EventHub supports a wide range of payment methods including all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For certain regions, we also support bank transfers and local payment options. All payments are processed securely through our PCI-compliant payment system.

EventHub offers multiple promotion tools to help you reach your target audience. You can create custom social media posts, email campaigns, and promotional codes directly from your event dashboard. Our platform also includes SEO optimization for better searchability, and featured event opportunities for additional visibility. Check out our Event Marketing Guide in the Resources section for more tips.

Refund policies are set by event organizers, not EventHub directly. Each event can have its own refund policy which is displayed during the ticket purchasing process. As an organizer, you can set your preferred refund policy when creating your event. Options include fully refundable up to a certain date, partially refundable, or non-refundable. We recommend being clear about your policy to maintain good relationships with attendees.
