Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about EventHub

Account & Registration

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar. Fill in your details such as name, email, and password. Verify your email address by clicking on the link sent to your inbox, and you're all set to start using EventHub.

You can browse events and view event details without an account. However, to create events, register for events, or use advanced features, you'll need to create a free account. This helps us provide a more personalized experience and secure your event data.

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Sign In" button, then select "Forgot Password?" link below the login form. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Creating & Managing Events

After signing in, click on the "Create" button in the navigation menu. Fill out the event details such as name, date, time, location, and description. You can also add custom fields, ticket types, and promotional images. Once complete, click "Create Event" to publish your event.

Yes, you can edit most details of your event after publishing. Navigate to your event dashboard, select the event you want to modify, and click on "Edit Event." Keep in mind that certain changes, especially to date, time, or location, will send notifications to registered attendees.

To cancel an event, go to your event dashboard, select the event, and click on "Manage Event." In the event settings, you'll find a "Cancel Event" option. You'll be asked to provide a reason for cancellation, which will be included in notifications to registered attendees. Keep in mind that cancellation policies regarding refunds apply.

Tickets & Registration

When creating or editing your event, navigate to the "Tickets" section. Here you can create different ticket types (e.g., General Admission, VIP, Early Bird), set prices, and establish available quantities. You can also create free tickets or donation-based options if your event doesn't have a fixed price.

EventHub accepts major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay. Event organizers can choose which payment methods to enable for their events. All payments are processed securely through our payment providers.

After registering and completing payment, attendees receive an email confirmation with their ticket(s) attached as a PDF. Tickets include a unique QR code for check-in. Attendees can also access their tickets through their EventHub account under "My Tickets." For convenience, we offer options to add the event to calendars and download tickets to mobile wallet apps.

Pricing & Billing

EventHub offers a range of pricing plans to meet different needs. Our Basic plan is free and allows you to create events with up to 50 attendees. For larger events, our Pro ($29/month) and Premium ($99/month) plans offer advanced features, higher attendee limits, and more customization options. Visit our Pricing page for detailed information.

For paid events, there is a standard processing fee of 2.5% + $0.99 per transaction. This fee covers payment processing costs. Premium plan subscribers enjoy reduced fees of 2% + $0.99 per transaction. You can choose to absorb these fees or pass them on to attendees during checkout.

Payments from ticket sales are processed according to your payment schedule. By default, funds are disbursed 5 days after your event. Pro and Premium subscribers can opt for earlier payouts (weekly or bi-weekly). To receive payments, you need to set up your payout method in your account settings, which can be a bank account or PayPal.
